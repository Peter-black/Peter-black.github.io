About

Equinox is a 3rd person runner where the obstacles are generated by the music.

You play as a firebird flying through the world as it falls apart around you to the music of your choice.

The game is targeted at mobile and html5 platforms and was developed by a team of students from Abertay University.

The game was developed using Unreal Engine 4. My job on this project was to create the gameplay systems such as obstacle spawning, lanes, movement and more.

Working on this project has been a great way to learn to work with artists, designers and other coders in a team.
