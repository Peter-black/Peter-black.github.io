ONRUSH - JUNE 2018

ONRUSH is a vehicular combat based "racing" game released by Codemasters.

I worked as part of the Core-Tech team on this project.

I started work on the Entity and Vehicle Customisation systems, over time I took responsibility for these areas.

On top of this I integrated new Havok physics features into the in-house engine. I also worked closely with artists to help create the ability visuals within the game.
